About Us

We serve the best biryani in Town. We have been serving authentic Indian cuisine and catering services for years. As a Biryani House, we offer a blend of traditional Hyderabadi Dum Biryani, Vijayawada Special Biryani's, and innovative Indian dishes. You will experience the Most Authentic Hyderabadi Biryani which perfectly combines the ancient traditions and authentic flavors of India.

From the moment you step into our restaurant, making your whole experience a fulfilling, satisfying, and relaxing one is our only obsession.

We pride ourselves on serving delicious meals that rival fine dining but doing so in a fun, casual environment and at reasonable prices.
